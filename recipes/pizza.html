Oven Made Margherita Pizza

Description

This is my beloved recipe for a home style pizza you could made at home to impress your friends and family

Ingrediants for the pizza dough

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 cup warm water (110째F/43째C)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Toppings needed

(feel free to add any more toppings to your pizza)

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • 8 oz (225g) fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Steps to Assemble the Pizza

1. Prepare the dough

  1. In a small bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until the mixture becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Mix the ingredients until a dough forms. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise for about 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.

2. Preheat the oven

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature it can go (usually around 500째F/260째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

3. Roll out the dough

  1. Punch down the risen dough and turn it out onto a floured surface. Roll it out into your desired pizza shape and thickness.

4. Assemble the Pizza

  1. Carefully transfer the rolled-out dough onto a parchment paper-lined pizza peel or an inverted baking sheet.
  2. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  3. Place the sliced mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  4. Arrange the tomato slices on the cheese. Season with salt and pepper.

5. Bake the pizza

  1. If using a pizza stone, slide the parchment paper with the assembled pizza onto the preheated stone in the oven. If not using a stone, simply place the baking sheet with the pizza in the oven.
  2. Bake for about 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

6. Finish the pizza

  1. Wear oven mitts or any other sufficiant gloves to take your pizza out the oven
  2. Once the pizza is out of the oven, sprinkle fresh basil leaves over the top.
  3. Drizzle a bit of olive oil over the pizza for extra flavor.

7. Profit

  1. Let the pizza cool slightly before slicing into wedges. Serve and enjoy your homemade Margherita pizza!

Feel free to customize the toppings according to your preferences. Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza straight from the oven!
